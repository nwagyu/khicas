11.1.4  Converting units: convert =>

The convert command (see Section 5.23.26) can convert a unit object into another compatible unit. For this:

  • convert takes two arguments:
    • unitobj, a unit object.
    • u, a unit compatible with unitobj.
  • convert(unitobj,u) returns unitobj in terms of u.

Recall that the => operator is the infixed version of convert.

Examples

  • Input:
    convert(1_h,_s)
    Output:
    3600.0 s
  • Input:
    convert(3600_s,_h)
    Output:
    1.0 h
