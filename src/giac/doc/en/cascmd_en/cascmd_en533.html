5.49.6  LU decomposition (for TI compatibility): LU

The LU command finds the LU decomposition of a matrix.

  • LU takes four arguments:
    • A, a numeric square matrix.
    • l,u and p, three variable names.
  • LU(A) returns the matrix P from the LU decomposition of A, and assigns L, U and P to the variables l,u and p. Namely, P is a permutation matrix, L is lower triangular with 1s on the diagonal, and U is upper triangular with PA=LU.
