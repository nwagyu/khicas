The Airy_Ai and Airy_Bi commands compute the Airy functions.

  • Airy_Ai and Airy_Bi take one argument:
    x, a real number.
  • Airy_Ai(x) and Airy_Bi(x) return the values of the Airy functions.

Examples

  • Input:
    Airy_Ai(1)
    Output:
      0.135292416313

    Airy_Bi(1)

      1.20742359495

    Airy_Ai(0)
