5.23.4  Putting together sine and cosine of the same angle: tcollect tCollect

The tcollect command linearizes trigonometric expressions (in terms of sin(n*x) and cos(n*x)) and combines sines and cosines of the same angle.
tCollect is a synonym for tcollect.

  • tcollect takes one argument:
    expr, an expression containing trigonometric functions.
  • tcollect(expr) returns expr after first linearizing it and then combining sines and cosines of the same angle.
