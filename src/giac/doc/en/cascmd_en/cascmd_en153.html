for x>1, and by its meromorphic continuation for x<1.

The Zeta command computes the ζ function.

  • Zeta takes one argument:
    x, a real number.
  • Zeta(x) returns the value of the ζ function ζ(x).

Examples

  • Input:
    Zeta(2)
    Output:
      
    π2
    6
  • Input:
    Zeta(4)
    Output:
      
    π4
    90
