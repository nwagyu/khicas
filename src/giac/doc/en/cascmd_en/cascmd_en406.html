5.38.5  The union of intervals: union

In Xcas, the union of two intervals is their convex hull.

The union operator is a binary infixed operator that can find the union of two intervals.

Examples

  • Input:
    i[1,3] union i[2,4]
    Output:
      [1.00000000000000..4.00000000000000]
  • Input:
    i[2,4] union i[6,9]
    Output:
      [2.00000000000000..9.00000000000000]
