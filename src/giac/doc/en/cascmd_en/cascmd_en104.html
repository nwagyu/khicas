5.5.10  Euclidean quotient and Euclidean remainder of two integers: iquorem

The iquorem command finds both the quotient and remainder of two integers (see Section 5.5.8).

  • iquorem takes two arguments:
    a and b, integers.
  • iquorem(a,b) returns the list [q,r], where q is the quotient and r the remainder of a divided by b.

Examples

  • Input:
    iquorem(148,5)
    Output:
      ⎡
    ⎣    		29,3⎤
    ⎦
  • Input:
    iquorem(25+12*i,5+7*i)
    Output:
      ⎡
    ⎣    		3−2 i,−4+i⎤
    ⎦
