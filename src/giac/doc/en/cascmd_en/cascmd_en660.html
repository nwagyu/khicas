8.3.11  Producing random numbers with the beta distribution: randbetad

The randbetad command finds random numbers chosen according to the beta distribution (see Section 8.4.12).

  • randbetad takes two arguments:
    a and b, positive real numbers (the parameters).
  • randbetad(a,b) returns a number chosen randomly according to the beta distribution with parameters a and b.

Example

Input:

randbetad(2,3)

Output (for example):

0.524453873081
