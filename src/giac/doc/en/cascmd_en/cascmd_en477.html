5.45.2  Addition and subtraction of two matrices: + - .+ .-

The infixed operators + and .+ (resp. - and .-) are used for the addition (resp. subtraction) of two matrices.

Examples

  • Input:
    [[1,2],[3,4]] + [[5,6],[7,8]]
    Output:
    ⎡
    ⎢
    ⎣
    68
    1012
    		⎤
    ⎥
    ⎦
    Input:
    [[1,2],[3,4]] - [[5,6],[7,8]]
    Output:
    ⎡
    ⎢
    ⎣
    −4−4
    −4−4
    		⎤
    ⎥
    ⎦


Remark.
+ and - can be used as prefixed operators; in this case they must be quoted, ’+’ and ’-’ (see Section 5.42.3 and Section 5.42.4).

Examples

  • Input:
    ’+’([[1,2],[3,4]],[[5,6],[7,8]],[[2,2],[3,3]])
    Output:
    ⎡
    ⎢
    ⎣
    810
    1315
    		⎤
    ⎥
    ⎦
  • Input:
    ’-’([[1,2],[3,4]],[[5,6],[7,8]])
    Output:
    ⎡
    ⎢
    ⎣
    −4−4
    −4−4
    		⎤
    ⎥
    ⎦
