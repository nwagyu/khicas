5.32.1  Numerator: getNum

The getNum command finds the numerator of an unreduced rational function.

  • getNum takes one argument:
    rat, a rational function.
  • getNum(rat) returns the numerator of rat.

Unlike numer (see Section 5.32.2), textttgetNum does not simplify the expression before extracting the numerator.

Examples

  • Input:
    getNum((x^2-1)/(x-1))
    Output:
      x2−1
  • Input:
    getNum((x^2+2*x+1)/(x^2-1))
    Output:
      x2+2 x+1
