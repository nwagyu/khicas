5.7.5  Numerator and denominator of a fraction: f2nd fxnd

The f2nd command finds the numerator and denominator of a fraction.
fxnd is a synonym for f2nd.

  • f2nd takes one argument:
    r, a fraction.
  • f2nd(r) returns the list of the numerator and denominator of r after it has been reduced to lowest terms. (For rational expressions see Section 5.32.5).

Example

Input:

f2nd(42/12)

Output:

⎡
⎣		7,2⎤
