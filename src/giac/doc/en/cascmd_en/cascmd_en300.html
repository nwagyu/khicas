5.27.12  Evaluating polynomials: peval polyEval

The peval command evaluates polynomials.
polyEval is a synonym for peval.

  • peval takes two arguments:
    • P, a polynomial given by the list of its coefficients.
    • a, a real number.
  • peval(P,a) returns the exact or numeric value of P(a), calculated using Horner’s method.

Examples

  • Input:
    peval([1,0,-1],sqrt(2))
    Output:
      √
    2
    		 √
    2
    		−1
    then input:
    normal(sqrt(2)*sqrt(2)-1)
    Output:
      1
  • Input:
    peval([1,0,-1],1.4)
    Output:
      0.96
