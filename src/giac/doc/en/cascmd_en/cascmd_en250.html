5.23.3  Increasing the phase by π/2 in a trigonometric expression: shift_phase

The shift_phase command increases the phase of a trigonometric expression by π/2.

  • shift_phase takes one argument:
    expr, a trigonometric expression.
  • shift_phase(expr) returns expr with the phase increased by π/2 (after automatic simplification).
