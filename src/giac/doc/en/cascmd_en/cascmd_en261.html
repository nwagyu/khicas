5.23.14  Transforming tan(x) into sin(x)/cos(x): tan2sincos

The tan2sincos command replaces tan(x) by sin(x)/cos(x) in an expression.

  • tan2sincos takes one argument:
    expr, an expression containing trigonometric functions.
  • tan2sincos(expr) returns expr with anything of the form tan(x) replaced by sin(x)/cos(x).

Example

Input:

tan2sincos(tan(2*x))

Output:
