5.12.24  Sub-expression of an expression: part

The part command finds subexpressions of an expression. (See Section 3.3.2.)

  • part takes two arguments:
    • expr, an expression.
    • n, an integer.
  • part(expr,n) evaluates expr and then returns the nth sub-expression of expr.


Examples

  • Input:
    part(x^2+x+1,2)
    Output:
    x
  • Input:
    part(x^2+(x+1)*(y-2)+2,2)
    Output:
    ⎛
    ⎝    		x+1⎞
    ⎠    		⎛
    ⎝    		y−2⎞
    ⎠
  • Input:
    part((x+1)*(y-2)/2,2)
    Output:
    y−2
