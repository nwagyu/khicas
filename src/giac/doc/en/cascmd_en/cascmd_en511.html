5.47.15  Subspace generated by the rows of a matrix: rowspace

The rowspace command finds a basis for the row space of a matrix.

  • rowspace takes one mandatory argument and one optional argument:
    • A, a matrix.
    • Optionally, var, a variable name.
  • rowspace(A ⟨var⟩) returns a list of vectors which form a basis of the subspace generated by the rows of A. With the optional argument var, Xcas will store the dimension of the subspace generated by the rows of A.
