5.5.7  The divisors of a number: idivis divisors

The idivis command finds the divisors of an (ordinary) integer.
divisors is a synonym for idivis.

  • idivis takes one argument:
    n, an integer or list of integers.
  • idivis(n) returns the list of the divisors of the integer n (or of a list of such lists).

Examples

  • Input:
    idivis(36)
    Output:
      ⎡
    ⎣    		1,2,3,4,6,9,12,18,36⎤
    ⎦
  • Input:
    idivis([36,22])
    Output:
      ⎡
    ⎣    		⎡
    ⎣    		1,2,3,4,6,9,12,18,36⎤
    ⎦    		,⎡
    ⎣    		1,2,11,22⎤
    ⎦    		⎤
    ⎦
