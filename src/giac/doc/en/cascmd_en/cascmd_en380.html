5.34.13  GCD in ℤ/pℤ[x]: gcd

The gcd command finds the greatest common divisor of two polynomials with coefficients in ℤ/pℤ (for prime p). (See also Section 5.5.1 and Section 5.28.5.)

  • gcd takes two arguments:
    P and Q, two polynomials with coefficients in ℤ/pℤ (p must be prime).
  • gcd(P,Q) returns the GCD of P and Q computed in ℤ/pℤ[x]

Example

Input:

gcd((2*x^2+5)%13,(5*x^2+2*x-3)%13)

Output:

⎛
⎝		1%13⎞
⎠		x+2%13
