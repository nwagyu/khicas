5.48.3  Eigenvectors: egv eigenvectors eigenvects eigVc

The egv command finds the eigenvectors of a diagonalizable matrix.
eigenvectors, eigenvects and eigVc are synonyms for egv.

  • egv takes one argument:
    A, a square matrix.
  • egv(A) returns a matrix whose columns are the eigenvectors of the matrix A if A is diagonalizable, otherwise it will fail.

See also Section 5.48.5 for characteristic vectors.
