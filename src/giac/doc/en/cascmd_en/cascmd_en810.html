13.7.9  The altitude of a triangle: altitude

The altitude command finds the altitude line of a triangle.

  • altitude takes three arguments:
    a,b,c, three points.
  • altitude(a,b,c) returns and draws the altitude line to the triangle with vertices a,b,c, through a and perpedicular to the segment from b to c.
