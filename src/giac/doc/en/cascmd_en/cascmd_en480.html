5.45.5  Cumulated sum of elements of each column of a matrix: cumSum

The cumSum command finds the cumulated sum of each column of a matrix (see also Section 5.40.27).

  • cumSum takes one argument:
    A, a matrix.
  • cumSum(A) returns the matrix whose columns are the cumulated sum of the elements of the corresponding column of the matrix A.

Example

Input:

cumSum([[1,2],[3,4],[5,6]])

Output:
