5.34.10  Computing an mod p: powmod powermod

For integers a,n and p, the powmod finds an modp.
powermod is a synonym for powmod.

  • powmod takes three arguments:
    a,n and p, integers.
  • powmod(a,n,p) returns an mod p in [0,p−1].

Examples

  • Input:
    powmod(5,2,13)
    Output:
    12
  • Input:
    powmod(5,2,12)
    Output:
    1
