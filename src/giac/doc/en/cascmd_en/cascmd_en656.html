8.3.7  Producing random numbers with Student’s distribution: randstudent

The randstudent command finds random numbers chosen according to Student’s distribution (see Section 8.4.8).

  • randstudent takes one argument:
    n, an integer (the degrees of freedom).
  • randstudent(n) returns a number chosen randomly according to Student’s distribution with n degrees of freedom.

Example

Input:

randstudent(5)

Output (for example):

0.268225314184
