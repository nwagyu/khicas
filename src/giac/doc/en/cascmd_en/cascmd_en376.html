5.34.9  Power in ℤ/pℤ and in ℤ/pℤ[x]: ^

The ^ operator raises modular numbers and polynomials to powers in ℤ/pℤ. (See also Section 5.8.2.) For polynomial expressions, use the normal command to simplify. Xcas uses the binary power algorithm to compute this.

Examples

  • Input:
    (5%13)^2
    Output:
    ⎛
    ⎝    		−1⎞
    ⎠    		%13
  • Input:
    normal(((2*x+1)%13)^5)
    Output:
    ⎛
    ⎝    		6%13⎞
    ⎠    		x5+⎛
    ⎝    		2%13⎞
    ⎠    		x4+⎛
    ⎝    		2%13⎞
    ⎠    		x3+⎛
    ⎝    		1%13⎞
    ⎠    		x2+⎛
    ⎝    		⎛
    ⎝    		−3⎞
    ⎠    		%13⎞
    ⎠    		x+1%13
    because 10=−3 (mod 13), 40=1 (mod 13), 80=2 (mod 13), 32=6 (mod 13).
