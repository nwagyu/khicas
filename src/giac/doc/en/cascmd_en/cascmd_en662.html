8.3.13  Producing random numbers with the exponential distribution: randexp

The randexp command finds random numbers chosen according to the exponential distribution (see Section 8.4.15).

  • randexp takes one argument:
    λ, a positive real number (the parameter).
  • randexp(λ) returns a number chosen randomly according to the exponential distribution with parameter λ.

Example

Input:

randexp(2.1)

Output (for example):

0.0288626239833
