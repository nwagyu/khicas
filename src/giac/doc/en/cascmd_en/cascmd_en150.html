The igamma command computes the lower incomplete γ function.

  • igamma takes two mandatory arguments and one optional argument:
    • a, a number.
    • b, a positive real number.
    • Optionally, the number 1.
  • igamma(a,b) returns γ(a,b).
  • igamma(a,b,1) returns a normalized version of the function; namely γ(a,b)/Γ(a).

Examples

  • Input:
    igamma(4.0,3.0)
    Output:
      2.11660866731
  • Input:
    igamma(4.0,3.0,1)
    Output:
      0.352768111218
    since Γ(4) = 6 and 2.11660866731/6 = 0.352768111218.
