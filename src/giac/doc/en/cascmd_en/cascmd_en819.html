13.9.2  Rhombuses in the plane: rhombus

See Section 14.8.2 for rhombuses in space.

The rhombus command creates rhombuses.

  • rhombus takes three mandatory arguments and two optional arguments.
    • A,B, two points.
    • a, a real number.
    • Optionally, varc, vard, two variable names.
  • rhombus(A,B,a ⟨varc,vard⟩) returns and draws the rhombus ABCD, where a is the counterclockwise angle from AB to AC. If the arguments varc and vard are given, then C and D will be assigned to them.
