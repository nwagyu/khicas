14.15.1  Cones: cone

The cone command creates cones.

  • cone takes three arguments:
    • A, a point.
    • v, a direction vector.
    • θ, a real number.
  • cone(A,v,θ) returns and draws the cone with vertex A, opening in the direction v with an aperture of 2θ.

Example

Input:

cone([0,1,0],[0,0,1],pi/3)
