The ugamma command computes the upper incomplete γ function.

  • ugamma takes two arguments:
    • a, a number.
    • b, a positive real number.
  • ugamma(a,b) returns the value of Γ(a,b).

Examples

  • Input:
    ugamma(3.0,2.0)
    Output:
      1.35335283237
  • Input:
    ugamma(-1.3,2)

      0.0142127568837
