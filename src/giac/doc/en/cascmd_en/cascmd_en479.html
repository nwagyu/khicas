5.45.4  Addition of elements of a column of a matrix: sum

The sum command (see also Section 5.20.3) can add the elements of the columns of a matrix.

  • sum takes one argument:
    A, a matrix.
  • sum(A) returns the list whose elements are the sum of the elements of each column of the matrix A.

Example

Input:

sum([[1,2],[3,4]])

Output:

⎡
⎣		4,6⎤
⎦
