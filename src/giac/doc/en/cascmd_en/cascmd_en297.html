5.27.9  Polynomial valuation: valuation ldegree

The valuation of a polynomial is the lowest degree of its non-zero monomials. The valuation command finds the valuation of a polynomal.
ldegree is a synonym for valuation.

  • valuation takes one argument:
    P, a polynomial given by a symbolic expression or by the list of its coefficients.
  • valuation(P) returns the valuation of P.

Examples

  • Input:
    valuation(x^3+x)
    Output:
      1
  • Input:
    valuation([1,0,1,0])
    Output:
      1
