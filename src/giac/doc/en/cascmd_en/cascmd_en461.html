5.42.2  Normalizing a vector: normalize unitV

The normalize command finds the unit vector in the direction of a given vector.
unitV is a synonym for normalize.

  • normalize takes one argument:
    V, a vector (list).
  • normalize(V) normalizes this vector for the l2 norm (the square root of the sum of the squares of its coordinates).

Example

Input:

normalize([3,4,5])
