5.5.26  Jacobi symbol: jacobi_symbol

Where the left hand side is the Jacobi symbol and the right hand side contains Legendre symbols. The jacobi_symbol command computes the Jacobi symbol.

  • jacobi_symbol takes two arguments:
    a and n, integers.
  • jacobi_symbol(a,n) returns the Jacobi symbol (a/n).

Examples

  • Input:
    jacobi_symbol(25,12)
    Output:
      1
  • Input:
    jacobi_symbol(35,12)
    Output:
      −1
  • Input:
    jacobi_symbol(33,12)
    Output:
      0
