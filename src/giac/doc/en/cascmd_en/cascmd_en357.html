5.32.2  Numerator after simplification: numer

The numer command finds the numerator of a rational function, after it has been reduced. (See also Section 5.7.3.)

  • numer takes one argument:
    rat, a rational function.
  • numer(rat) returns the numerator of the irreducible representation of rat.

Examples

  • Input:
    numer((x^2-1)/(x-1))
    Output:
    x+1
  • Input:
    numer((x^2+2*x+1)/(x^2-1))
    Output:
    x+1
